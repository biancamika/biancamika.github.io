##### Introduction
## A safe space for students to be in the spotlight

The Canvas is a dance showcase series featuring the students of the dance studio Nude Floor. 
‚Äç
Nowadays, most student showcases take a top-down approach, where teachers create routines for their students to perform. In contrast, The Canvas organizers took a **bottom-up** approach, where students conceptualized and choreographed their pieces on their own. We simply acted as **curators**, bringing all these students into one space.

For the past three volumes (from early 2024 to now), I've led graphic design for the Canvas.
